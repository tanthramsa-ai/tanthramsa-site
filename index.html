The problem

Most lenders are still running the book by hand.

Spreadsheets, disconnected apps, and manual follow-ups don't scale — and they don't show you what's actually happening in the field.

Slow disbursals

Every new loan means re-keying data across tools, so approvals and payouts drag on longer than they should.

Leaked collections

Without a shared system, cash collected in the field doesn't always make it cleanly onto the books.

No real-time view

Leadership finds out how the portfolio is doing at month-end — long after problems could have been caught.

Why LendersHub

A single, secure system of record.

Replace the spreadsheets and side-tools with one branded platform that covers the full lending lifecycle.

Faster growth

Onboard a new lender or branch in minutes. Launch weekly, daily, monthly, or term products without custom development.

Stronger collections

Field agents get daily and overdue lists, capture payments with photo receipts, and work offline — cash isn't missed in low-network areas.

Lower risk & leakage

Role-based controls from Owner to Viewer, OTP-secured logins, and a full transaction ledger create accountability and a clean audit trail.

Real-time visibility

Leadership sees active loans, collections, and portfolio health as they happen — not at month-end.

Everything inside LendersHub

One platform, the whole lending lifecycle.

From onboarding a customer to closing a loan, every step runs through the same ledger — so the office, the field, and leadership are always looking at the same numbers.

01 / Loan management

Any loan product, no development required

Configure weekly, daily, monthly, or term loan products directly in the platform — interest structure, tenure, fees, and repayment schedule are all set up, not coded. Launch a new product for a branch in the time it takes to fill in a form.

Product builderFlexible schedulesMulti-branch rollout
02 / Collections & field agents

Built for the last mile, not just the back office

Field agents open the app to a daily list and an overdue list, capture payments with photo receipts, and keep working when there's no signal — everything syncs the moment connectivity returns. Nothing collected in the field goes unrecorded.

Offline modePhoto receiptsRoute listsAuto-sync
03 / Roles & security

Accountability from Owner to Viewer

Every user gets a role — Owner, Admin, Branch Manager, Agent, Viewer — that defines exactly what they can see and touch. OTP-secured logins and a full transaction ledger mean every action is traceable back to a person.

Role-based accessOTP loginAudit trail
