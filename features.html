Everything inside LendersHub

One platform, the whole lending lifecycle.

From onboarding a customer to closing a loan, every step runs through the same ledger — so the office, the field, and leadership are always looking at the same numbers.

01 / Loan management

Any loan product, no development required

Configure weekly, daily, monthly, or term loan products directly in the platform — interest structure, tenure, fees, and repayment schedule are all set up, not coded. Launch a new product for a branch in the time it takes to fill in a form.

Product builderFlexible schedulesMulti-branch rollout
02 / Collections & field agents

Built for the last mile, not just the back office

Field agents open the app to a daily list and an overdue list, capture payments with photo receipts, and keep working when there's no signal — everything syncs the moment connectivity returns. Nothing collected in the field goes unrecorded.

Offline modePhoto receiptsRoute listsAuto-sync
03 / Roles & security

Accountability from Owner to Viewer

Every user gets a role — Owner, Admin, Branch Manager, Agent, Viewer — that defines exactly what they can see and touch. OTP-secured logins and a full transaction ledger mean every action is traceable back to a person.

Role-based accessOTP loginAudit trail
04 / Reporting & real-time visibility

The portfolio, as it happens

Leadership sees active loans, collections, and portfolio health update in real time instead of waiting for a month-end report. Drill from a branch-level summary down to a single transaction without leaving the dashboard.

Live dashboardsBranch drill-downExportable reports
05 / Multi-tenant administration

Run every client from one console

Each lender operates in a fully isolated, branded environment. As the platform owner, you manage every client, subscription tier, and invoice centrally — onboarding a new lender takes minutes, not a project.

Isolated tenantsWhite-label brandingCentral billing
06 / Web & mobile

The right interface for the right job

Office staff and leadership work from a web portal built around approvals, configuration, and oversight. Agents in the field carry a purpose-built mobile app. Both read from and write to the same ledger.

Web portalMobile appShared data model
